Test Script: TS-005 - Registration Without Accepting Terms and Conditions

GIVEN a visitor is on the registration page
WHEN the visitor fills all required fields correctly
BUT does NOT check the "Accept Terms and Conditions" checkbox
AND clicks "Register" button
THEN the system should display error "You must accept the Terms and Conditions"
AND prevent form submission

EXPECTED RESULT:
- No account created
- Error message displayed near checkbox
- Checkbox highlighted in red
