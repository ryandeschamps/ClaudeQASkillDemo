Test Script ID: TS-010
Test Scenario: Birth of Child
Priority: Critical
Preconditions:
- Active employee enrolled in benefits with spouse
- Life event for birth configured in system
- 60-day enrollment window from birth date configured
- Birth certificate upload capability enabled
- Medical plan allows adding newborn

GIVEN: An employee (Lisa Jackson) has a baby born on 2024-09-10
WHEN: The employee reports the birth and adds newborn to benefit plans within 60 days
THEN: The newborn is successfully added and enrolled in coverage from date of birth

Test Steps:
1. Log into self-service portal as employee (lisa.jackson@company.com)
2. Navigate to "Report Life Event" section
3. Select "Birth of Child" as life event type
4. Enter birth date of child: 2024-09-10
5. Verify system displays 60-day enrollment window (expires 2024-11-09)
6. Click "Continue" to start life event enrollment
7. In "Add Dependent" section, click "Add New Dependent"
8. Enter newborn information: Name (Noah Jackson), Relationship (Child), Date of Birth (2024-09-10)
9. Save dependent record
10. Verify system creates action item for birth certificate upload
11. Navigate to benefit elections within life event process
12. Review current medical coverage: Gold PPO Employee + Spouse + 2 Children
13. Verify system automatically includes newborn in family coverage
14. Review updated family coverage with 3 children now included
15. Confirm monthly premium for family coverage ($680.00)
16. Review dental plan and verify newborn included
17. Submit life event enrollment elections
18. Verify coverage effective date set to birth date (2024-09-10)
19. Upload birth certificate document
20. Verify document submission and "Pending Approval" status
21. Check confirmation showing newborn coverage from date of birth

Expected Result:
- Birth life event successfully reported and processed
- Newborn (Noah Jackson) added as dependent
- Medical coverage automatically includes newborn under existing family plan
- Premium remains $680.00 (family rate covers all children)
- Coverage effective date is birth date (2024-09-10) ensuring no coverage gap
- Action item for birth certificate created with status "Pending Approval"
- Employee notified of successful enrollment and document requirements
- Retroactive coverage confirmed from date of birth
- System allows 60-day enrollment window for birth life event
