Test Script ID: TS-026
Test Scenario: Open Enrollment - Drop Plans
Priority: High
Preconditions:
- Active open enrollment period
- Employee currently enrolled in multiple voluntary benefits
- System allows dropping voluntary benefits during open enrollment
- Required minimum benefits maintained (cannot waive core benefits without attestation)
- Premium reduction calculated correctly

GIVEN: An employee (Larry Cook) wants to reduce benefit costs by dropping vision and dental coverage during open enrollment
WHEN: The employee removes voluntary plans from their enrollment
THEN: Selected plans successfully dropped and premium reduced for next plan year

Test Steps:
1. Employee logs into self-service portal during open enrollment (larry.cook@company.com)
2. Navigate to Open Enrollment section
3. Click "Start Open Enrollment" or "Make Changes"
4. Review current elections:
    - Bronze PPO Medical Employee + Children ($440.00)
    - Enhanced Dental Family ($68.00)
    - Standard Vision Family ($34.00)
5. Employee decides to keep medical but drop dental and vision
6. Navigate to Dental coverage section
7. Click "Remove" or "Drop Coverage" for dental plan
8. System prompts: "Are you sure you want to drop dental coverage?"
9. Confirm removal of dental coverage
10. Navigate to Vision coverage section
11. Click "Remove" or "Drop Coverage" for vision plan
12. System prompts: "Are you sure you want to drop vision coverage?"
13. Confirm removal of vision coverage
14. Review enrollment summary showing:
    - Bronze PPO Medical Employee + Children (KEEPING)
    - Enhanced Dental Family (DROPPED)
    - Standard Vision Family (DROPPED)
15. Review reduced monthly premium: $440.00 (down from $542.00)
16. System displays savings: $102.00 per month
17. Submit open enrollment elections
18. Verify dental and vision coverage end date: 2024-12-31
19. Verify medical coverage continues: 2025-01-01 forward
20. Receive confirmation email showing dropped benefits and premium reduction

Expected Result:
- Employee successfully drops dental and vision coverage
- System requires confirmation before removing benefits
- Enrollment summary clearly shows which plans are dropped
- Monthly premium correctly reduced from $542.00 to $440.00
- Dental coverage ends 2024-12-31
- Vision coverage ends 2024-12-31
- Medical coverage continues unchanged
- Savings amount clearly communicated ($102.00 monthly)
- Confirmation email shows updated benefit elections for 2025
- Payroll updated with reduced premium for new year
- Employee understands dropped benefits cannot be re-added until next enrollment period
