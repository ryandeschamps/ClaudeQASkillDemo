Test Scenario: Open Enrollment - Change Medical Plan
Priority: Critical
Preconditions:
- Active open enrollment period

- Plan comparison tools functional
- Employee currently enrolled in medical plan
- Rate information for all plans available

GIVEN: An employee (Amanda Wright) wants to compare medical plans and switch during open enrollment
WHEN: The employee uses plan comparison tools and changes medical plan for next year
THEN: New medical plan successfully elected with coverage effective first day of new plan year

Test Steps:
1. Employee logs into self-service portal during open enrollment (amanda.wright@company.com)
2. Navigate to Open Enrollment section
3. Click "Start Open Enrollment" or "Make Changes"
4. Review current medical plan: Gold PPO Employee Only ($185.00)

10. Review plan decision support tools or recommendations

13. Verify coverage level: Employee + Spouse (adding spouse during OE)

21. Receive confirmation email with new plan details and ID card information

- Employee successfully changes from Gold PPO to Silver HMO
