Test Script ID: TS-008
Test Scenario: New Hire Waive Coverage
Priority: Medium
Preconditions:
- Waive coverage option available for medical plans
- Employee has alternative coverage (spouse's plan, parent's plan, etc.)
- System allows waiving medical while electing other benefits
- Waiver reason codes configured in system
- Employee attestation or waiver form process established

GIVEN: A new employee has medical coverage through their spouse's employer
WHEN: The employee waives medical coverage while enrolling in other available benefits
THEN: Medical coverage is successfully waived with documented reason, and employee enrolls in other desired benefits

Test Steps:
1. Log into self-service portal as new hire
2. Navigate to Benefits Enrollment section
3. View new hire enrollment period and deadline
4. Select Medical plan category
5. Review available medical plan options
6. Click "Waive Coverage" or "Decline Medical Coverage" option
7. System prompts for waiver reason - select "Covered by spouse's plan"
8. Enter spouse's coverage information (carrier name, policy number if required)
9. Review waiver attestation statement confirming alternative coverage
10. Accept attestation by checking acknowledgment box
11. Sign electronic waiver form if required
12. Confirm medical waiver submission
13. Proceed to enroll in other benefit categories (Dental, Vision, Life, etc.)
14. Select and enroll in Dental plan with Employee Only coverage
15. Enroll in Vision plan with Employee Only coverage
16. Enroll in Basic Life Insurance (default employer-provided)
17. Review enrollment summary showing medical as "Waived" with other enrollments active
18. Submit all benefit elections including waiver
19. Verify confirmation message acknowledging medical waiver and other enrollments
20. Check that premium deduction only includes non-waived benefits

Expected Result:
- Medical coverage successfully waived with documented reason
- Waiver attestation completed and stored in system
- Employee enrolled in Dental, Vision, and Life Insurance successfully
- Monthly premium reflects only non-waived benefits
- Waiver status clearly indicated as "Waived" in enrollment summary
- Employee notified of waiver confirmation and future enrollment opportunities
- System documents that waiver was voluntary and employee has alternative coverage
- Employee understands they cannot re-enroll in medical until next enrollment period or qualifying life event

Test Data Reference:
- Variant IDs: Any variant with Coverage_Level showing waiver or separate plan elections
- Test Data: Create test data showing:
  - Medical: Waived
  - Dental: Employee Only coverage
  - Vision: Employee Only coverage
  - Life Insurance: Basic coverage
  - Premium: Reduced amount (no medical premium)

Notes:
- Verify system requires reason for waiving coverage
- Confirm waiver attestation language is clear and legally compliant
- Test that employee can revoke waiver only during qualifying life events
- Verify annual reminder to reconsider waived coverage during open enrollment
- Ensure waiver does not affect eligibility for other benefits
- Test that evidence of alternative coverage is captured if required by company policy
- Confirm employee cannot waive employer-provided basic life insurance if mandatory
- Verify waiver documentation retained for audit and compliance purposes
