Test Script ID: TS-003
Test Scenario: New Hire Multiple Dependents
Priority: Critical
Preconditions:
- New hire enrolled in system with valid hire date
- Employee has access to self-service benefits portal
- Dependent management functionality enabled
- Document upload capability functional
- Medical, dental plans allow family coverage
- Birth certificates and marriage certificates can be uploaded

GIVEN: A new employee (Jennifer Williams) with spouse and two children needs to enroll family in benefits
WHEN: The employee adds multiple dependents and enrolls them in benefit plans during new hire enrollment
THEN: All dependents are successfully added and enrolled with appropriate coverage and documentation requirements

Test Steps:
1. Log into self-service portal as new hire (jennifer.williams@company.com)
2. Navigate to "Add Dependent" or "Contacts and Dependents" section
3. Click "Add New Dependent" button
4. Enter spouse information: Name (Mark Williams), Relationship (Spouse), Date of Birth
5. Save spouse dependent record
6. Click "Add New Dependent" again for first child
7. Enter child information: Name (Sophie Williams), Relationship (Child), Date of Birth
8. Save first child dependent record
9. Click "Add New Dependent" for second child
10. Enter child information: Name (Jake Williams), Relationship (Child), Date of Birth
11. Save second child dependent record
12. Navigate to Benefits Enrollment section
13. Select Medical plan category and choose "Platinum HMO"
14. Select coverage level "Employee + Family" (covers employee, spouse, and all children)
15. Verify all three dependents appear in coverage list
16. Review monthly premium for family coverage ($625.00)
17. Add Dental plan with family coverage
18. Review enrollment summary with all dependents listed
19. Submit enrollment elections
20. Verify action items created for required documents (marriage certificate, birth certificates)
21. Upload marriage certificate for spouse
22. Upload birth certificates for both children
23. Verify documents submitted successfully and marked "Pending Approval"

Expected Result:
- All three dependents (spouse and 2 children) successfully added to employee record
- Family coverage selected and confirmed for Platinum HMO medical plan
- Monthly premium correctly calculated at $625.00 for family coverage
- Action items generated for required dependent verification documents
- Document upload successful with status "Pending Approval"
- Coverage effective date set to hire date (2024-02-10)
- All dependents listed on enrollment confirmation
- Confirmation email sent with enrollment details and action item reminders
