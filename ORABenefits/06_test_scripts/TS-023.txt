Test Script ID: TS-023
Test Scenario: Open Enrollment - Keep Current Plans
Priority: Critical
Preconditions:
- Active open enrollment period configured
- Employee has existing benefit elections from previous year
- Passive enrollment rules configured (keep same benefits if no action)
- Open enrollment communications sent to all employees
- System displays current elections for review

GIVEN: An active employee (Joseph King) with current benefit enrollments during open enrollment period
WHEN: The employee reviews current benefits and confirms to keep same coverage for next year
THEN: Current benefit elections successfully carry forward to next plan year

Test Steps:
1. Benefits administrator configures and opens annual open enrollment period
2. Open enrollment period: 2024-11-01 to 2024-11-30
3. System sends notifications to all eligible employees
4. Employee logs into self-service portal (joseph.king@company.com)
5. Dashboard displays "Open Enrollment Active - Make Your Elections"
6. Click "Start Open Enrollment" or "Review Benefits"
7. System displays current benefit elections from prior year
8. Review current medical plan: Gold PPO Employee Only ($185.00)
9. Review current dental plan: Basic Dental Employee Only
10. Review current life insurance: Basic Life $50,000
11. See option buttons: "Keep Current Plans" or "Make Changes"
12. Review any rate changes for next year displayed
13. Review plan document links and provider networks
14. Click "Keep Current Plans" or "Confirm Elections"
15. System displays confirmation summary of passive enrollment
16. Review and acknowledge passive enrollment confirmation
17. Submit passive enrollment confirmation
18. Verify confirmation message: "Your benefits will continue for 2025"
19. Receive confirmation email with benefit summary for upcoming year
20. Verify new plan year effective date: 2025-01-01

Expected Result:
- Employee accesses open enrollment successfully
- Current benefit elections displayed clearly
- Option to keep current plans prominently available
- Rate changes for next year communicated
- Employee successfully confirms to keep current elections
- Passive enrollment processed and confirmed
- All current benefits carry forward to new plan year (2025-01-01)
- Monthly premium for next year communicated ($185.00 or adjusted rate)
- Confirmation email sent with benefit summary
- Employee does not need to re-elect FSA or make active elections
- Enrollment status: "Completed - Passive Enrollment"
