Test Script ID: TS-053
Test Scenario: Designate Primary Beneficiary
Priority: Critical
Preconditions:
- Employee enrolled in life insurance plan
- Beneficiary designation functionality enabled
- Beneficiary types configured (Primary, Contingent)
- Percentage validation enabled
- Electronic signature capability functional

GIVEN: An employee with life insurance coverage needs to designate their spouse as 100% primary beneficiary
WHEN: The employee completes beneficiary designation for life insurance
THEN: Spouse successfully designated as 100% primary beneficiary with designation electronically signed

Test Steps:
1. Employee logs into self-service portal
2. Navigate to "Benefits" section
3. Select "Beneficiaries" or "Life Insurance Beneficiaries"
4. View current beneficiary status: "No beneficiaries designated"
5. System displays prompt: "Please designate beneficiaries for your life insurance coverage"
6. Click "Add Primary Beneficiary" or "Designate Beneficiaries"
7. System displays beneficiary designation form
8. Beneficiary type pre-selected: "Primary Beneficiary"
9. Enter beneficiary information:
   - First Name: Laura
   - Middle Name: Marie
   - Last Name: Adams
   - Relationship: Spouse
   - Date of Birth: 1987-04-15
   - Social Security Number: XXX-XX-XXXX
   - Address: Same as employee (checkbox)
   - Phone: (555) 234-5678
10. Enter beneficiary percentage: 100%
11. System validates percentage equals 100% for all primary beneficiaries
12. Review life insurance coverage details:
    - Policy: Basic Life Insurance
    - Coverage Amount: $50,000
    - Beneficiary: Laura Adams (Spouse) - 100%
13. Click "Save Beneficiary"
14. System displays beneficiary summary:
    - Primary Beneficiaries: Laura Adams (Spouse) 100%
    - Contingent Beneficiaries: None designated
15. System prompts: "Would you like to add contingent beneficiaries?" (optional)
16. Select "Skip" to proceed with primary only
17. Review complete beneficiary designation
18. System displays electronic signature acknowledgment:
    - "I designate the above beneficiary(ies) for my life insurance coverage"
    - "I understand I can change beneficiaries at any time"
19. Enter electronic signature (type full name)
20. Click "Submit Designation"
21. Confirmation message: "Beneficiary designation completed successfully"
22. Confirmation email sent with beneficiary details
23. Beneficiary designation effective immediately

Expected Result:
- Employee successfully accesses beneficiary designation functionality
- Beneficiary form includes all necessary fields for person identification
- Relationship types appropriate for beneficiaries (spouse, child, parent, sibling, trust, other)
- Percentage field validates that total equals 100%
- Primary beneficiary (spouse) successfully designated at 100%
- System prevents submission if percentages don't equal 100%
- Beneficiary designation applies to all life insurance coverage
- Electronic signature requirement satisfied
- Acknowledgment language clear about changeability
- Beneficiary designation confirmed and saved
- Employee receives confirmation with beneficiary details
- Designation effective immediately
- Employee can update or change beneficiaries anytime
