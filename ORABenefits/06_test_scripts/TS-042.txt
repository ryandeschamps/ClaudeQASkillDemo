Test Script ID: TS-042
Test Scenario: Administrator Assign Benefits Relationship
Priority: High
Preconditions:
- Benefits administrator role with relationship assignment privileges
- Employee with complex assignment scenario (multiple legal entities, cost centers, etc.)
- Benefits relationship assignment rules configured
- Manual assignment override capability enabled
- Audit logging for relationship assignments

GIVEN: An employee works across multiple legal entities and automatic benefits relationship assignment fails
WHEN: Benefits administrator manually assigns appropriate benefits relationship
THEN: Benefits relationship successfully assigned, employee has correct benefit eligibility and plan options

Test Steps:
1. Identify employee with benefits relationship assignment issue
2. Example: Employee works 50% for ORA Corp and 50% for ORA International
3. Automatic assignment logic unable to determine primary benefits relationship
4. Benefits administrator logs into Oracle Fusion HCM
5. Navigate to employee's work relationship or assignment details
6. Review employment assignments:
   - Assignment 1: ORA Corp, 20 hours/week
   - Assignment 2: ORA International, 20 hours/week
7. Navigate to "Benefits Relationship" section
8. Current status shows: "No Benefits Relationship Assigned" or "Assignment Failed"
9. Click "Manually Assign Benefits Relationship"
10. System displays available benefits groups:
   - BG-001 (ORA Corp benefits)
   - BG-002 (ORA International benefits)
11. Review benefits comparison for each option
12. Consider employee preference and HR policy
13. Select benefits relationship: BG-001 (ORA Corp)
14. Enter assignment reason: "Employee elected ORA Corp benefits - dual assignment scenario"
15. Set assignment effective date: Employee's hire date or current date
16. Review eligibility rules for selected benefits group
17. Confirm employee meets eligibility criteria for assigned group
18. Submit benefits relationship assignment
19. System assigns BG-001 to employee
20. Verify employee now has access to BG-001 benefit plans
21. Verify eligibility rules from BG-001 apply to employee
22. Employee can now enroll in benefits under assigned relationship
23. Audit trail captures: assignment, benefits group, reason, administrator, date

Expected Result:
- Administrator successfully accesses benefits relationship assignment
- Employee's complex work situation clearly displayed
- Available benefits groups and eligibility rules shown
- Administrator can compare benefits options
- Manual assignment completed with documented reason
- Benefits relationship assigned: BG-001 (ORA Corp)
- Assignment effective date appropriately set
- Employee's benefit eligibility determined by assigned benefits group
- Employee can access and enroll in correct benefit plans
- Enrollment uses rates and rules from assigned benefits group
- Assignment reason documented for audit and review
- Audit trail captures all assignment details
- Employee's benefits record shows assigned relationship clearly

Test Data Reference:
- Variant IDs: V050, V051, V052, V053, V054 (Multiple Assignments)
- Test Data: Employees with Benefits_Relationship: Multiple Assignments
  - Complex work arrangements requiring manual assignment
  - Various scenarios: multiple legal entities, part-time across divisions, etc.

Notes:
- Verify complex assignment scenarios identified for manual review
- Confirm benefits comparison helps informed decision-making
- Test that assignment considers HR policy and employee circumstances
- Verify eligibility rules from assigned benefits group properly applied
- Ensure assignment reason documented for justification
- Test that enrollment uses correct rates and rules post-assignment
- Confirm assignment can be changed if circumstances change
- Verify audit trail adequate for compliance review
- Test that multiple assignment scenarios have clear resolution path
- Ensure employee communication about benefits group assignment
