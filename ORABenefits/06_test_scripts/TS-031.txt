Test Script ID: TS-031
Test Scenario: Open Enrollment - Extend Window
Priority: High
Preconditions:
- Active open enrollment period approaching deadline
- Benefits administrator has authority to extend enrollment period
- Technical capability to modify enrollment period dates
- Communication plan for notifying employees of extension
- System allows date changes during active enrollment

GIVEN: Open enrollment scheduled to close on 2024-11-30 but needs one-week extension due to employee requests
WHEN: Benefits administrator extends open enrollment period by one week to 2024-12-07
THEN: Enrollment period successfully extended and all employees notified of new deadline

Test Steps:
1. Benefits administrator reviews open enrollment participation rates on 2024-11-25
2. Identifies lower than expected participation (many employees haven't completed)
3. Receives requests from HR and employees for extension due to holidays, technical issues, etc.
4. Decision made to extend open enrollment by one week
5. Benefits administrator logs into Oracle Fusion HCM
6. Navigate to "Open Enrollment Configuration"
7. Select current open enrollment period (2024-11-01 to 2024-11-30)
8. Click "Edit" or "Modify Enrollment Period"
9. Change end date from 2024-11-30 to 2024-12-07
10. System validates new end date is after original end date
11. Save modified enrollment period
12. System prompts: "Notify all participants of extended deadline?"
13. Click "Yes" to trigger automated notifications
14. System sends emails to all eligible employees:
    - Subject: "Open Enrollment Extended - New Deadline December 7"
    - Message explains extension and new deadline
15. Update open enrollment banners and portal messages with new date
16. Verify employees can still access open enrollment after original deadline (2024-11-30)
17. Test enrollment submission on 2024-12-01 (after original deadline, before new deadline)
18. Verify enrollment processes successfully
19. Monitor participation rates during extension period
20. Close open enrollment on new deadline: 2024-12-07 at 11:59 PM
21. Run final enrollment reports with extended deadline results

Expected Result:
- Open enrollment period successfully extended by one week
- New end date: 2024-12-07 (extended from 2024-11-30)
- System accepts date modification without data loss or issues
- All employees receive notification of extension via email
- Portal displays updated deadline prominently
- Employees can continue making enrollment elections during extension
- Enrollments submitted during extension period process successfully
- No impact on previously submitted enrollments
- Final enrollment deadline honored: 2024-12-07 at 11:59 PM
- Participation rates improve during extension period
- Default enrollment processing delayed to accommodate extension
- All communications updated with new deadline

Test Data Reference:
- Applies to all eligible employees
- Monitor participation metrics before and after extension
- Track enrollments during extension period

Notes:
- Verify administrator has authority to extend enrollment periods
- Confirm system allows date modifications during active enrollment
- Test that previous enrollments remain intact during extension
- Verify new deadline consistently displayed across all interfaces
- Ensure automated notifications sent to all eligible employees
- Test that system hard-stops enrollment after new deadline
- Confirm payroll and carrier deadlines accommodate extension
- Verify downstream processes (billing, carrier feeds) adjusted for extension
- Consider communicating reason for extension to employees
- Document extension decision and justification for audit purposes
