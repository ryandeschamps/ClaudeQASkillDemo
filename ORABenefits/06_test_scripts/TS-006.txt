Test Script ID: TS-006
Test Scenario: New Hire Administrator-Assisted Enrollment
Priority: High
Preconditions:
- Benefits administrator has appropriate security role and permissions
- Administrator access to enroll on behalf of employees enabled
- New hire record exists in HCM system
- Employee unable to complete self-service enrollment (no computer access, disability, etc.)
- All benefit plans active and available

GIVEN: A new employee (Gregory Sanchez) lacks computer access and needs benefits administrator to enroll them in benefits
WHEN: Benefits administrator processes enrollment on behalf of employee using administrator functions
THEN: Administrator successfully enrolls employee in selected benefit plans with proper documentation of administrator-assisted enrollment

Test Steps:
1. Log into Oracle Fusion HCM as Benefits Administrator
2. Navigate to "Benefits Administrator" work area or dashboard
3. Select "Enroll Participant" or "Administrator Enrollment" function
4. Search for new hire employee by name or person number (P100055, Gregory Sanchez)
5. Verify employee's new hire eligibility status and enrollment period end date
6. Select "Start New Enrollment" or "Initiate Administrator Enrollment"
7. System prompts to select enrollment reason: Choose "New Hire Enrollment"
8. Review available benefit plans for employee based on benefits group (BG-001)
9. Gather employee's benefit elections (in person, phone, or paper form)
10. Select Silver HMO medical plan with Employee Only coverage per employee's choice
11. Review and confirm monthly premium ($180.00)
12. Add any additional benefit elections per employee's wishes
13. Review enrollment summary with employee verbally or via paper confirmation
14. Enter administrator notes: "Administrator-assisted enrollment - employee lacks computer access"
15. Submit enrollment elections on behalf of employee
16. Verify confirmation message and enrollment confirmation number
17. Print or email enrollment confirmation to employee
18. Document administrator-assisted enrollment in employee file
19. Create task to follow up with employee for signature if required by policy

Expected Result:
- Employee successfully enrolled in Silver HMO with Employee Only coverage
- Monthly premium of $180.00 correctly assigned
- Enrollment marked as "Administrator-Assisted" in system
- Enrollment effective date set to hire date (2024-04-10)
- Administrator notes captured in enrollment record
- Enrollment confirmation generated and provided to employee
- Employee can view enrollment in self-service portal once access obtained
- Audit trail shows administrator as enrolling party
