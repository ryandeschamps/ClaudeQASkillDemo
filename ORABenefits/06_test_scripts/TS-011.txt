Test Script ID: TS-011
Test Scenario: Adoption
Priority: High
Preconditions:
- Active employee enrolled in benefits
- Adoption life event configured in system
- 60-day enrollment window from adoption date configured
- Adoption papers upload capability enabled
- Medical and dental plans allow adding adopted children

GIVEN: An employee (Steven Martin) adopts a child with adoption finalized on 2024-07-28
WHEN: The employee reports the adoption and adds adopted child to benefit plans within 60 days
THEN: The adopted child is successfully added and enrolled in coverage from adoption placement date

Test Steps:
1. Log into self-service portal as employee (steven.martin@company.com)
2. Navigate to "Report Life Event" section
3. Select "Adoption" as life event type
4. Enter adoption placement date: 2024-07-28 (date child placed with employee)
5. Verify 60-day enrollment window displayed
6. Click "Continue" to begin life event enrollment
7. Click "Add New Dependent" in dependent section
8. Enter adopted child information: Name (Oliver Martin), Relationship (Child), Date of Birth
9. Mark relationship type as "Adopted Child"
10. Save dependent record
11. Verify action item created for adoption papers
12. Navigate to benefit plan elections
13. Review current coverage: Bronze PPO Employee Only
14. Change coverage level to "Employee + Children"
15. Review updated premium ($430.00 monthly)
16. Add child to dental coverage
17. Review enrollment summary with adopted child included
18. Submit life event enrollment
19. Verify coverage effective date set to adoption placement date (2024-07-28)
20. Upload adoption papers or court order documents
21. Mark document as "Adoption Papers"
22. Verify document uploaded with "Pending Approval" status

Expected Result:
- Adoption life event successfully reported in system
- Adopted child (Oliver Martin) added as dependent with adoption relationship
- Coverage changed from Employee Only to Employee + Children
- Bronze PPO premium updated to $430.00
- Coverage effective date is adoption placement date (2024-07-28)
- Action item for adoption papers created and document uploaded
- Retroactive coverage applied from placement date
- Employee receives confirmation of coverage changes
- 60-day enrollment window enforced from adoption date
