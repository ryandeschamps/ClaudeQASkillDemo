Test Script ID: TS-019
Test Scenario: Salary Change Triggering New Eligibility
Priority: High
Preconditions:
- Employee receives salary increase due to promotion
- Executive or enhanced benefits tied to salary thresholds
- Salary change triggers eligibility re-evaluation
- System monitors compensation changes for benefits impact
- Enhanced benefit plans configured with eligibility rules

GIVEN: An employee (Sarah Allen) receives a promotion with significant salary increase on 2024-09-20
WHEN: The salary change triggers eligibility evaluation and employee qualifies for executive benefits
THEN: Employee is notified of new benefit eligibility and can enroll in enhanced benefits

Test Steps:
1. HR processes promotion for employee (sarah.allen@company.com) in HCM
2. Update employee salary effective 2024-09-20
3. New salary crosses threshold for executive benefits eligibility
4. System automatically evaluates benefits eligibility based on new compensation
5. System identifies employee now qualifies for executive benefits
6. System triggers "Salary Change - New Eligibility" life event
7. Notification sent to employee about new benefit eligibility
8. Employee logs into self-service portal
9. Reviews notification of new benefit eligibility
10. Navigate to "Available Benefits" or "Enroll in New Benefits"
11. Review newly available benefit options (executive life insurance, enhanced disability, etc.)
12. Review current coverage: Gold PPO Employee Only ($190.00)
13. Review new executive benefit options with enhanced coverage
14. Decide to add supplemental executive life insurance ($250,000 coverage)
15. Review premium for executive life insurance
16. Enroll in executive disability insurance
17. Review enrollment summary with new executive benefits
18. Submit elections for newly available benefits
19. Verify coverage effective date aligned with salary change (2024-09-20)
20. Receive confirmation of new executive benefit enrollments

Expected Result:
- Salary change successfully processed with effective date 2024-09-20
- System automatically evaluates benefits eligibility
- Employee identified as newly eligible for executive benefits
- Salary change life event triggered
- Employee receives notification of new eligibility within 5 business days
- Employee can access and review executive benefit options
- Employee successfully enrolls in executive life and disability insurance
- Coverage effective date matches salary change date (2024-09-20)
- Premium for new benefits added to payroll deductions
- Existing benefits (Gold PPO) remain unchanged unless employee chooses to upgrade
- Confirmation sent with all benefit details
