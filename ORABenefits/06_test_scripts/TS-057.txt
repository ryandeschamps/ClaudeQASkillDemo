Test Script ID: TS-057
Test Scenario: Update Beneficiary Anytime
Priority: High
Preconditions:
- Employee has existing beneficiary designations
- Beneficiary updates allowed outside enrollment periods
- No restrictions on beneficiary change frequency
- Electronic signature required for changes
- Change tracking and audit trail configured

GIVEN: An employee with existing beneficiary designation needs to update beneficiaries due to changed circumstances
WHEN: The employee updates beneficiary designations outside of enrollment periods
THEN: Beneficiary changes successfully processed anytime and effective immediately

Test Steps:
1. Employee experiences life change: divorce (no longer wants ex-spouse as beneficiary)
2. Employee logs into self-service portal (outside open enrollment or life event period)
3. Navigate to "Benefits" section
4. Click "Beneficiaries" or "Life Insurance Beneficiaries"
5. Review current beneficiary designation:
   - Primary: Former Spouse - 100%
   - Contingent: None
6. Click "Update Beneficiaries" or "Change Beneficiaries"
7. System displays current beneficiaries with edit options
8. Remove former spouse as primary beneficiary:
   - Click "Remove" or "Delete" next to ex-spouse name
9. System prompts: "Are you sure you want to remove this beneficiary?"
10. Confirm removal
11. Add new primary beneficiaries:
    - Click "Add Primary Beneficiary"
    - Add Son: Marcus Johnson, 50%
    - Add Daughter: Aisha Johnson, 50%
12. System validates new percentages: 50% + 50% = 100% âœ“
13. Optionally add contingent beneficiaries:
    - Add Mother: Dorothy Johnson, 100% contingent
14. Review updated beneficiary designation:
    - PRIMARY:
      - Marcus Johnson (Son) - 50%
      - Aisha Johnson (Daughter) - 50%
    - CONTINGENT:
      - Dorothy Johnson (Mother) - 100%
15. System shows comparison:
    - OLD: Former Spouse 100%
    - NEW: Two children 50% each, Mother contingent
16. Electronic signature required for beneficiary change
17. Acknowledgment: "I understand this change is effective immediately"
18. Type full name to sign
19. Submit beneficiary change
20. System processes change immediately (no waiting period)
21. Confirmation: "Beneficiary designation updated successfully"
22. Effective date: Immediately (today's date)
23. Receive confirmation email with updated beneficiaries
24. Previous beneficiary designation archived in history

Expected Result:
- Employee can access and update beneficiaries anytime (24/7/365)
- No restriction to enrollment periods or life events for beneficiary changes
- Current beneficiaries clearly displayed for reference
- Employee can remove existing beneficiaries
- Employee can add new beneficiaries
- Percentage validation ensures new total equals 100%
- System shows before/after comparison for clarity
- Electronic signature required to confirm changes
- Beneficiary changes effective immediately upon submission (no delay)
- No waiting period or processing time
- Confirmation shows effective date is immediate
- Previous beneficiary designation archived for audit trail
- Change history tracked: old beneficiaries, new beneficiaries, date, reason if provided
- Updated beneficiaries apply to all life insurance coverage immediately

Test Data Reference:
- Any employee with existing beneficiary designations
- Common change scenarios:
  - Divorce (remove ex-spouse, add children or other family)
  - Birth of child (add new child)
  - Death of beneficiary (replace with new beneficiary)
  - Marriage (add new spouse)

Notes:
- Verify beneficiary changes allowed anytime (not restricted to enrollment periods)
- Confirm electronic signature required for all beneficiary changes
- Test immediate effectiveness (no waiting period)
- Verify change history comprehensive for audit
- Ensure previous designations archived, not deleted
- Test that multiple changes in short period all tracked separately
- Confirm employees can change beneficiaries as frequently as needed
- Verify no life event required to change beneficiaries
- Test that beneficiary changes don't require administrator approval
- Ensure change confirmation includes effective date
