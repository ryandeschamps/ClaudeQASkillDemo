Test Script ID: TS-032
Test Scenario: Open Enrollment - Intervening Life Event
Priority: High
Preconditions:
- Active open enrollment period
- Employee has started but not completed open enrollment
- Qualifying life event occurs during open enrollment period
- System handles concurrent life event and open enrollment
- Both enrollment types processed without conflict

GIVEN: An employee (Roger Simmons) is in middle of open enrollment when they get married on 2024-10-08
WHEN: The employee reports marriage life event during open enrollment period
THEN: Life event processed immediately allowing spouse addition, and open enrollment elections preserved

Test Steps:
1. Open enrollment period active: 2024-11-01 to 2024-11-30
2. Employee logs in and starts open enrollment on 2024-11-01 (roger.simmons@company.com)
3. Employee reviews plans but doesn't submit final elections yet
4. Employee gets married on 2024-10-08
5. Employee returns to benefits portal on 2024-10-10
6. Clicks "Report Life Event" while open enrollment still in progress
7. System displays message: "Open enrollment is active. Life event will be processed immediately."
8. Select life event type: "Marriage"
9. Enter marriage date: 2024-10-08
10. System processes life event separately from open enrollment
11. Employee adds spouse as dependent: Elizabeth Simmons
12. Upload marriage certificate
13. Select medical plan change due to life event: Gold PPO Employee + Spouse
14. Review life event enrollment summary
15. Submit life event enrollment
16. System confirms life event processed with effective date 2024-10-08
17. Return to open enrollment elections
18. System shows updated current elections including spouse from life event
19. Continue with other open enrollment changes for 2025 plan year if desired
20. Submit open enrollment elections
21. Verify two separate enrollment records: one for life event (effective 2024-10-08) and one for open enrollment (effective 2025-01-01)

Expected Result:
- System recognizes concurrent open enrollment and life event
- Life event processed immediately with mid-year effective date
- Open enrollment elections preserved and separate from life event
- Employee successfully adds spouse via marriage life event
- Spouse coverage effective from marriage date (2024-10-08)
- Gold PPO premium for Employee + Spouse starts immediately
- Open enrollment elections can be completed separately
- Open enrollment reflects current state after life event processed
- Two distinct enrollment records maintained:
  - Life event enrollment effective 2024-10-08
  - Open enrollment effective 2025-01-01
- No conflict between life event and open enrollment processing
- Employee receives two confirmations: one for life event, one for open enrollment
- Premium adjustments handled correctly for both events
