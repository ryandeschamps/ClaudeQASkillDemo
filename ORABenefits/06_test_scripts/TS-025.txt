Test Script ID: TS-025
Test Scenario: Open Enrollment - Add New Plans
Priority: Critical
Preconditions:
- Active open enrollment period
- Employee currently enrolled in basic benefits
- Additional voluntary plans available (vision, disability, supplemental life)
- Employee not currently enrolled in these voluntary plans
- Plan information and rates available

GIVEN: An employee (Charles Lopez) wants to expand coverage by enrolling in vision and disability during open enrollment
WHEN: The employee enrolls in previously unelected voluntary benefit plans
THEN: New benefit plans successfully added with coverage effective first day of new plan year

Test Steps:
1. Employee logs into self-service portal during open enrollment (charles.lopez@company.com)
2. Navigate to Open Enrollment section
3. Click "Start Open Enrollment" or "Make Changes"
4. Review current elections: Platinum HMO Medical and Basic Dental only
5. Navigate to "Available Benefits" or "Add Coverage"
6. View list of voluntary benefits not currently elected
7. Select "Vision" plan category
8. Review vision plan options: Standard Vision and Premium Vision
9. Select Standard Vision plan
10. Choose coverage level: Employee + Children (2 children)
11. Review vision premium and benefits (eye exams, glasses, contacts)
12. Click "Add to Cart" or "Enroll"
13. Navigate to Disability Insurance section
14. Review Short-term Disability and Long-term Disability options
15. Select Long-term Disability insurance
16. Review coverage details and premium ($62.00 monthly)
17. Complete health questions if required for disability coverage
18. Click "Add to Cart"
19. Review enrollment summary showing all elections:
    - Platinum HMO Medical (existing)
    - Basic Dental (existing)
    - Standard Vision (NEW)
    - Long-term Disability (NEW)
20. Review total monthly premium with new additions
21. Submit open enrollment elections
22. Verify all new coverage effective date: 2025-01-01

Expected Result:
- Employee successfully accesses voluntary benefit options
- Vision plan options clearly displayed with rates and coverage details
- Employee enrolls in Standard Vision for self and children
- Disability insurance options clearly explained
- Employee enrolls in Long-term Disability ($62.00 monthly)
- Health questions completed if required (disability may need evidence of insurability)
- Enrollment summary shows both existing and new plans
- All coverage effective date: 2025-01-01
- Total monthly premium calculated including new benefits
- Confirmation email sent with all benefit details
- New benefit ID cards issued for vision coverage
- Payroll updated to include premiums for new benefits
