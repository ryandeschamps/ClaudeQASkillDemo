Test Script ID: TS-028
Test Scenario: Open Enrollment - Update Beneficiaries
Priority: High
Preconditions:
- Active open enrollment period
- Employee enrolled in life insurance with existing beneficiary designations
- Beneficiary update functionality available during open enrollment
- System allows multiple primary and contingent beneficiaries
- Beneficiary percentage validation enabled

GIVEN: An employee (Eric Adams) wants to review and update life insurance beneficiaries during open enrollment
WHEN: The employee updates beneficiary designations to reflect current family situation
THEN: Beneficiary information successfully updated and confirmed for life insurance policies

Test Steps:
1. Employee logs into self-service portal during open enrollment (eric.adams@company.com)
2. Navigate to Open Enrollment section
3. Click "Start Open Enrollment" or "Make Changes"
4. Review current life insurance coverage: Basic Life $50,000
5. Click "Review Beneficiaries" or "Update Beneficiaries"
6. Review current beneficiary designation:
    - Primary: Laura Adams (Spouse) - 100%
7. Decide to update beneficiaries due to birth of child
8. Click "Edit Beneficiaries"
9. Update primary beneficiaries:
    - Primary: Laura Adams (Spouse) - 60%
    - Primary: Sarah Adams (Child) - 20%
    - Primary: Michael Adams (Child) - 20%
10. System validates total percentage equals 100%
11. Add contingent beneficiaries:
    - Contingent: John Adams (Sibling) - 50%
    - Contingent: Mary Adams (Sibling) - 50%
12. System validates contingent percentages equal 100%
13. Review beneficiary relationship types (Spouse, Child, Sibling, Trust, Other)
14. Enter required information for each beneficiary: Full name, DOB, SSN, Address
15. Save beneficiary designations
16. Review confirmation summary of beneficiary updates
17. System displays: "These beneficiaries apply to: Basic Life Insurance $50,000"
18. If employee has supplemental life, prompted to update those beneficiaries separately
19. Submit beneficiary updates as part of open enrollment
20. Receive confirmation email with updated beneficiary information

Expected Result:
- Employee successfully accesses beneficiary management during open enrollment
- Current beneficiaries displayed clearly
- Employee updates primary beneficiaries with appropriate percentages
- System validates total percentages equal 100% for both primary and contingent
- Employee adds multiple beneficiaries for life insurance
- All beneficiary information captured: name, relationship, DOB, SSN, address
- Beneficiary updates apply to all life insurance policies or can be specified separately
- Confirmation summary shows updated beneficiary designations
- Updates effective 2025-01-01 with rest of open enrollment elections
- Confirmation email includes beneficiary update acknowledgment
- Beneficiary information stored securely
