Test Script ID: TS-060
Test Scenario: Record Individual Payment
Priority: High
Preconditions:
- Benefits billing charges generated for employee
- Employee paying via check or direct payment (not payroll deduction)
- Payment recording functionality enabled for administrators
- Payment methods configured (check, ACH, wire, credit card)
- Receipt generation capability functional

GIVEN: An employee on direct bill (not payroll deduction) sends check payment for benefit premiums
WHEN: Payroll or benefits administrator records the check payment against outstanding charges
THEN: Payment successfully recorded, applied to charges, and balance updated

Test Steps:
1. Employee on COBRA or leave of absence (direct bill method)
2. Outstanding charges for November 2024: $450.00
3. Employee mails check payment: Check #1234 for $450.00
4. Payroll administrator receives check on 2024-11-05
5. Administrator logs into Oracle Fusion HCM
6. Navigate to "Benefits Billing" or "Payment Processing"
7. Click "Record Payment" or "Enter Payment"
8. Search for employee: Name or Person Number
9. Employee: Robert Mitchell, Person Number P100044
10. Review outstanding charges:
    - Invoice #INV-2024-11-001
    - Amount due: $450.00
    - Due date: 2024-11-01
    - Status: Outstanding
11. Click "Record Payment"
12. Enter payment details:
    - Payment date: 2024-11-05
    - Payment method: Check
    - Check number: 1234
    - Payment amount: $450.00
    - Bank name: First National Bank (optional)
13. Select charges to apply payment:
    - Invoice #INV-2024-11-001: $450.00 (full amount)
14. Payment allocation: $450.00 to Invoice #INV-2024-11-001
15. Verify payment allocation equals payment amount
16. Enter optional notes: "Check received via mail"
17. Click "Submit Payment"
18. System validates payment information
19. Payment recorded: Payment ID PAY-2024-11-001
20. System automatically applies payment to invoice
21. Invoice status updated: "Paid"
22. Outstanding balance: $0.00
23. Payment confirmation displayed
24. Generate payment receipt for employee records
25. Email or mail receipt to employee
26. Check deposited and reconciled with bank

Expected Result:
- Administrator successfully records individual check payment
- Employee's outstanding charges clearly displayed
- Payment details captured: date, method, amount, check number
- Payment allocation to specific invoices specified
- System validates payment amount matches allocation
- Payment recorded with unique payment ID
- Payment automatically applied to outstanding charges
- Invoice status updated to "Paid"
- Outstanding balance accurately reflects payment
- Payment receipt generated for employee
- Transaction history shows payment applied
- Audit trail captures: payment details, administrator, date/time
- Payment available for bank reconciliation

Test Data Reference:

- Test Data: Row 44 (P100043, Edward Carter)
  - Terminated employee on COBRA
  - Payment_Method: Direct Bill

Notes:
- Verify various payment methods supported (check, ACH, wire, credit card)
- Confirm payment can be partially applied if less than full amount
- Test payment allocation across multiple invoices
- Verify overpayment handling (creates credit balance)
- Ensure receipt generation includes all payment details
- Test NSF (non-sufficient funds) reversal process
- Confirm payment reconciliation with bank statements
- Verify payment history accessible for audit
- Test that payment updates billing status in real-time
- Ensure integration with accounting system if applicable
