Test Script ID: TS-020
Test Scenario: Length of Service Milestone
Priority: High
Preconditions:
- Employee reaches service milestone (5 years)
- Service-based benefits configured in system
- System monitors hire date anniversaries
- Enhanced benefits available after service milestone
- Automatic eligibility evaluation enabled

GIVEN: An employee (Brian Young) reaches 5 years of service on 2024-03-10
WHEN: The system identifies the service milestone and triggers eligibility for service-based benefits
THEN: Employee is automatically eligible for enhanced benefits like vacation purchase or enhanced disability

Test Steps:
1. System monitors employee hire date anniversaries
2. Employee's hire date: 2014-03-10, reaching 10 years on 2024-03-10
3. System automatically identifies 10-year service milestone
4. System evaluates benefits eligibility tied to length of service
5. System identifies employee now qualifies for service-based benefits
6. System triggers "Length of Service Milestone" life event
7. Notification sent to employee about new benefit eligibility
8. Employee logs into self-service portal (brian.young@company.com)
9. Reviews notification: "Congratulations on 10 years of service!"
10. Navigate to "Available Benefits" section
11. Review newly available service-based benefits
12. See option for Short-term Disability insurance (now available after 5+ years)
13. See option for vacation purchase program (additional weeks available)
14. Review premium for short-term disability: $45.00 monthly
15. Enroll in short-term disability insurance
16. Review vacation purchase option and elect additional week
17. Review enrollment summary with new service-based benefits
18. Submit elections for newly available benefits
19. Verify coverage effective date aligned with service milestone (2024-03-10)
20. Receive confirmation and congratulatory message for service anniversary

Expected Result:
- System automatically identifies 10-year service milestone
- Length of service life event triggered automatically
- Employee receives notification of new eligibility with congratulatory message
- Service-based benefits clearly displayed and available for enrollment
- Employee successfully enrolls in short-term disability ($45.00 monthly)
- Employee enrolls in vacation purchase program
- Coverage effective date matches service milestone date (2024-03-10)
- Premium for new benefits added to payroll deductions
- Service anniversary recognized and celebrated
- Confirmation includes all new benefit details
