Test Script ID: TS-033
Test Scenario: Open Enrollment - Rate Comparison
Priority: Medium
Preconditions:
- Active open enrollment period
- Multiple plan options available with different rate structures
- Plan comparison tool configured and functional
- Cost calculator available showing annual vs. monthly costs
- Provider network lookup integrated with comparison tool

GIVEN: An employee (Janet Morgan) wants to make informed decision by comparing plan rates and total costs
WHEN: The employee uses rate comparison and cost calculator tools during open enrollment
THEN: Employee can effectively compare plans and make cost-based decision with full information

Test Steps:
1. Employee logs into self-service portal during open enrollment (janet.morgan@company.com)
2. Navigate to Open Enrollment section
3. Click "Start Open Enrollment" or "Compare Plans"
4. Current plan: Gold PPO Employee Only ($185.00/month)
5. Click "Compare All Medical Plans" or "Plan Comparison Tool"
6. System displays comparison table with all available medical plans:
    - Bronze PPO
    - Silver HMO
    - Gold PPO (current)
    - Platinum HMO
7. Comparison shows for each plan:
    - Monthly premium (employee portion)
    - Annual premium
    - Deductible (individual/family)
    - Out-of-pocket maximum
    - Primary care copay
    - Specialist copay
    - Emergency room coverage
    - Prescription drug coverage
    - Provider network (HMO vs. PPO)
8. Click "Cost Calculator" or "Total Cost Estimator"
9. Enter anticipated healthcare usage for next year:
    - Doctor visits: 4 per year
    - Specialist visits: 2 per year
    - Prescriptions: 3 ongoing medications
    - Anticipated procedures: None major planned
10. Calculator shows estimated total annual cost for each plan:
    - Bronze PPO: $2,800 premium + $1,200 out-of-pocket = $4,000 total
    - Silver HMO: $2,160 premium + $400 out-of-pocket = $2,560 total
    - Gold PPO: $2,220 premium + $300 out-of-pocket = $2,520 total (current)
    - Platinum HMO: $3,000 premium + $100 out-of-pocket = $3,100 total
11. Review recommendation: Gold PPO or Silver HMO provide best value for usage
12. Click on Silver HMO for detailed information
13. View provider network lookup - verify primary doctor in network
14. Compare coverage details between Gold PPO and Silver HMO
15. Decide to switch to Silver HMO based on cost analysis
16. Select Standard Vision Employee Only plan
17. Review final comparison: total monthly premium $180.00 (Silver HMO only)
18. Savings compared to current: $5.00/month or $60.00/year
19. Submit open enrollment election for Silver HMO

Expected Result:
- Plan comparison tool displays clear, side-by-side comparison of all plans
- All key plan features visible in comparison (premium, deductible, copays, network)
- Cost calculator provides personalized estimate based on anticipated usage
- Total annual cost calculated for each plan (premium + expected out-of-pocket)
- Recommendation provided based on employee's healthcare needs
- Provider network lookup integrated allowing verification of in-network doctors
- Employee can make informed, data-driven decision
- Detailed plan information accessible from comparison view
- Employee successfully selects Silver HMO based on cost analysis
- Estimated savings clearly communicated ($60/year)
- Confirmation shows new plan selection and rate
