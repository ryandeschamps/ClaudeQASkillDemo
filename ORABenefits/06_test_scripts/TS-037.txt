Test Script ID: TS-037
Test Scenario: Administrator Approve Documents
Priority: Critical
Preconditions:
- Benefits administrator role with document approval authority
- Employees have uploaded required documents
- Documents pending administrator review and approval
- Document viewing capability functional
- Approval workflow configured

GIVEN: Multiple employees have uploaded required documents (marriage certificates, birth certificates) pending approval
WHEN: Benefits administrator reviews and approves valid documents
THEN: Documents approved, action items closed, and coverage activated

Test Steps:
1. Benefits administrator logs into Oracle Fusion HCM
2. Navigate to "Action Items" or "Document Approvals"
3. Filter to show "Pending Approval" documents only
4. Review queue of documents awaiting approval
5. Select first document: Marriage certificate for employee Amanda Wilson
6. Click to view document details:
   - Employee: Amanda Wilson (P100002)
   - Document type: Marriage certificate
   - Upload date: 2024-01-25
   - Life event: Marriage
   - Status: Pending Approval
7. Click "View Document" to open uploaded image/PDF
8. Review document for authenticity and completeness:
   - Names match employee and spouse records
   - Date of marriage matches reported life event date
   - Issued by authorized government entity
   - Document is legible and complete
9. Verify document appears legitimate and unaltered
10. Check document against fraud indicators
11. Document passes verification checks
12. Click "Approve Document"
13. System prompts for approval notes (optional)
14. Enter notes: "Verified - document authentic, names and dates match"
15. Confirm approval
16. System updates document status to "Approved"
17. Action item automatically closed
18. If enrollment was suspended, coverage automatically activated
19. Employee receives notification: "Your marriage certificate has been approved"
20. Move to next document in queue
21. Repeat approval process for additional valid documents
22. Generate approval activity report showing documents processed

Expected Result:
- Administrator successfully accesses document approval queue
- Documents pending approval clearly listed with key details
- Document viewer displays uploaded images/PDFs clearly
- Administrator can thoroughly review document for authenticity
- Verification checklist available for consistency
- Document approval processed with one click
- Approval notes captured for audit trail
- Document status updates to "Approved" immediately
- Related action item automatically closed
- Suspended enrollments automatically activated upon approval
- Employee notified of approval status
- Coverage becomes effective per life event rules
- Approval activity tracked and reportable
- Audit trail captures administrator ID, approval date, and notes

Test Data Reference:
- Variant IDs: V002, V003, V004, V007 (Document_Status: Pending Approval to Approved)
- Test Data: Multiple rows with documents pending approval
  - Row 3 (P100002, Maria Rodriguez): Marriage Certificate
  - Row 4 (P100003, Michael Chen): Birth Certificate
  - Row 5 (P100004, Jennifer Williams): Birth Certificate
  - Row 8 (P100007, David Brown): Marriage Certificate
