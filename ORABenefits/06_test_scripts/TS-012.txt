Test Script ID: TS-012
Test Scenario: Divorce - Remove Spouse
Priority: Critical
Preconditions:
- Active employee enrolled with spouse coverage
- Divorce life event configured in system
- 60-day enrollment window from divorce date configured
- Divorce decree upload capability enabled
- COBRA notification process functional

GIVEN: An employee (Kevin Garcia) gets divorced with decree finalized on 2024-03-15
WHEN: The employee reports divorce and removes ex-spouse from benefit plans within 60 days
THEN: Ex-spouse is successfully removed from coverage, premium adjusted, and COBRA notification triggered

Test Steps:
1. Log into self-service portal as employee (kevin.garcia@company.com)
2. Navigate to "Report Life Event" section
3. Select "Divorce" as life event type
4. Enter divorce decree date: 2024-03-15
5. Review 60-day enrollment window
6. Click "Continue" to start life event enrollment
7. System displays current dependents including spouse
8. Review current coverage: Gold PPO Employee + Spouse ($395.00 premium)
9. Select option to remove spouse from coverage
10. Confirm removal of spouse (ex-spouse name) from dependent list
11. Update coverage level from "Employee + Spouse" to "Employee Only"
12. Review new premium amount ($195.00 for Employee Only)
13. Review other benefit plans (dental, life insurance) and remove spouse where applicable
14. Update life insurance beneficiary if spouse was listed
15. Review enrollment summary showing coverage changes and premium reduction
16. Submit life event enrollment elections
17. Verify coverage end date for ex-spouse (divorce date 2024-03-15)
18. Upload divorce decree document
19. Verify document uploaded with "Pending Approval" status
20. Confirm system generates COBRA notification for ex-spouse
21. Verify premium reduction reflected in payroll starting next cycle

Expected Result:
- Divorce life event successfully reported and processed
- Ex-spouse removed from all benefit coverage
- Coverage level changed from Employee + Spouse to Employee Only
- Monthly premium reduced from $395.00 to $195.00
- Ex-spouse coverage end date set to divorce date (2024-03-15)
- Divorce decree uploaded and marked "Pending Approval"
- COBRA notification automatically generated and sent to ex-spouse
- Life insurance beneficiary updated if spouse was previously designated
- Premium reduction processed through payroll
- Employee receives confirmation of all coverage changes
