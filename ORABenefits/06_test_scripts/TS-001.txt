Test Script ID: TS-001
Test Scenario: New Hire Initial Enrollment - Self Service
Priority: Critical
Preconditions:
- Employee hired with start date set in Oracle HCM
- Benefits eligibility rules configured for new hires
- Medical, dental, and life insurance plans active and available
- Self-service portal accessible to employee
- Employee has valid login credentials

GIVEN: A new employee (James Anderson) with hire date 2024-01-15 starts employment
WHEN: The employee accesses the self-service benefits portal to enroll in benefits during the initial enrollment period
THEN: The employee successfully enrolls in Gold PPO medical plan with coverage starting on hire date

Test Steps:
1. Log into Oracle Fusion HCM self-service portal as new hire (james.anderson@company.com)
2. Navigate to Benefits enrollment section from the home page
3. Verify that the system displays "New Hire Enrollment" as the enrollment type
4. Verify enrollment period end date is displayed (typically 31 days from hire date)
5. Review list of available benefit plans (Medical, Dental, Vision, Life Insurance)
6. Select "Medical" plan category and review available plan options
7. Select "Gold PPO" plan and choose "Employee Only" coverage level
8. Review premium amount ($185.00 monthly) and plan details
9. Click "Add to Cart" or "Enroll" button
10. Navigate to Life Insurance section and review default coverage (Basic Life $50,000)
11. Accept or modify life insurance coverage as needed
12. Review enrollment summary showing all selected plans and total monthly cost
13. Submit enrollment by clicking "Submit Elections" button
14. Verify confirmation message displays with enrollment confirmation number
15. Verify email confirmation sent to employee's email address

Expected Result:
- Employee successfully enrolled in Gold PPO medical plan with Employee Only coverage
- Monthly premium of $185.00 correctly calculated and displayed
- Enrollment status shows as "Enrolled" or "Completed"
- Coverage effective date matches hire date (2024-01-15)
- Confirmation email received with enrollment summary
- Action items section shows no pending requirements for employee-only coverage
