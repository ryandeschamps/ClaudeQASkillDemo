Test Script ID: TS-015
Test Scenario: Death of Dependent
Priority: Medium
Preconditions:
- Active employee with dependent enrolled in coverage
- Death of dependent life event configured
- 60-day enrollment window to make changes
- Death certificate upload capability enabled
- System handles sensitive life events appropriately

GIVEN: An employee (Joshua Lee) experiences the death of a dependent (Michelle Lee) on 2024-08-10
WHEN: The employee reports the death and removes dependent from coverage
THEN: Dependent is compassionately removed from coverage, premium adjusted, and appropriate notifications sent

Test Steps:
1. Employee or HR contacts benefits administrator to report death of dependent
2. Benefits administrator logs into Oracle Fusion HCM
3. Navigate to employee record (joshua.lee@company.com, P100027)
4. Access "Report Life Event" in administrator mode
5. Select "Death of Dependent" as life event type
6. Enter date of death: 2024-08-10
7. Select dependent: Michelle Lee (Spouse)
8. System displays 60-day window for benefit changes
9. Mark dependent status as "Deceased" in system
10. Navigate to benefit plan elections
11. Review current coverage: Gold PPO Employee + Spouse ($370.00)
12. System automatically removes deceased dependent from coverage
13. Update coverage level to "Employee Only"
14. Review new premium: $190.00 for Employee Only
15. Update life insurance beneficiary if deceased dependent was listed
16. Submit life event enrollment changes
17. Verify coverage end date for dependent set to date of death (2024-08-10)
18. Upload death certificate when available (may be delayed)
19. Administrator adds compassionate notes to record
20. Verify premium adjustment and potential refund calculation
21. System sends sensitive notification to employee confirming changes

Expected Result:
- Death life event handled with appropriate sensitivity and confidentiality
- Dependent (Michelle Lee) status updated to "Deceased"
- Dependent removed from all benefit coverage effective date of death (2024-08-10)
- Coverage level changed from Employee + Spouse to Employee Only
- Monthly premium reduced from $370.00 to $190.00
- Any overpaid premiums calculated for potential refund
- Life insurance beneficiary updated if deceased was previously designated
- Death certificate uploaded when available
- Employee receives compassionate communication of changes
- Premium reduction reflected in payroll
