Test Script ID: TS-009
Test Scenario: Marriage - Add Spouse
Priority: Critical
Preconditions:
- Active employee enrolled in benefits
- Life event functionality configured for marriage
- 31-day enrollment window from marriage date configured
- Marriage certificate upload capability enabled
- Medical and dental plans allow spouse coverage

GIVEN: An employee (Christopher Wilson) gets married on 2024-06-15
WHEN: The employee reports the marriage life event and adds spouse to medical and dental plans within 31 days
THEN: The spouse is successfully added as dependent and enrolled in coverage with proper effective date

Test Steps:
1. Log into self-service portal as active employee (christopher.wilson@company.com)
2. Navigate to "Report Life Event" or "Life Events" section
3. Click "Report New Life Event" button
4. Select life event type: "Marriage"
5. Enter marriage date: 2024-06-15
6. Review calculated life event enrollment window (31 days from marriage date)
7. Click "Continue" or "Start Life Event Enrollment"
8. Navigate to "Add Dependent" section within life event process
9. Click "Add New Dependent" and enter spouse information
10. Enter spouse details: Name (Amanda Wilson), Relationship (Spouse), Date of Birth
11. Save spouse dependent record
12. Verify action item created to upload marriage certificate
13. Proceed to benefit plan elections
14. Select Medical plan category and view current enrollment
15. Change coverage from "Employee Only" to "Employee + Spouse"
16. Review new plan: Gold PPO with spouse coverage ($395.00 monthly premium)
17. Select Dental plan and add spouse coverage
18. Review enrollment summary showing coverage changes and new premium
19. Submit life event enrollment elections
20. Navigate to action items and upload marriage certificate
21. Verify document uploaded and marked "Pending Approval"
22. Check enrollment status and coverage effective date

Expected Result:
- Marriage life event successfully reported and created in system
- Spouse (Amanda Wilson) added as dependent with relationship documented
- Medical coverage changed from Employee Only to Employee + Spouse
- Gold PPO premium increased to $395.00 monthly
- Dental coverage updated to include spouse
- Coverage effective date set to marriage date (2024-06-15)
- Action item created for marriage certificate with "Pending Approval" status
- Life event enrollment window displayed (expires 31 days after marriage)
- Confirmation email sent with enrollment changes and document requirements
