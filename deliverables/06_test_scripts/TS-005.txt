TEST SCRIPT: TS-005
Test Scenario: View Product Ratings and Reviews
User Story: As a Visitor, I want to read product ratings and reviews from other customers, so that I can assess product quality and make informed purchase decisions
Priority: High
Related Requirements: FR-005

GIVEN the user is viewing a product details page
  AND the product has approved customer reviews

WHEN the page loads

THEN the system should display:
  - Overall product rating (average star rating)
  - Total number of reviews
  - Individual customer reviews with:
    * Customer name (or anonymous identifier)
    * Star rating
    * Review text
    * Review date
  AND reviews should be sorted by most recent first
  AND pagination should be available if there are many reviews

WHEN the product has no reviews

THEN the system should display "No reviews yet" or similar message
  AND indicate that the user can be the first to review after purchase

EXPECTED RESULT:
- Ratings and reviews are displayed correctly
- Review information is complete and accurate
- Pagination works for large numbers of reviews
- Display is user-friendly and informative
- Only approved reviews are shown
