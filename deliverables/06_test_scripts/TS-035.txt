TEST SCRIPT: TS-035
Test Scenario: Receive Order Confirmation Email
User Story: As a Buyer who completed purchase, I want to receive an order confirmation email, so that I can have a record of my purchase and order details
Priority: High
Related Requirements: FR-008

GIVEN the user has successfully completed a purchase
  AND payment has been processed
  AND order has been created in the system

WHEN the order is confirmed

THEN the system should send an email to the user's registered email address
  AND the email should contain:
    - Order number
    - Order date
    - List of purchased items with quantities and prices
    - Billing address
    - Shipping address
    - Payment method used
    - Order total breakdown (subtotal, shipping, tax, total)
    - Expected delivery timeframe
    - Link to track order
    - Customer support contact information
  AND the email should be sent within minutes of order placement

WHEN the email is not delivered due to technical issues

THEN the system should retry sending
  AND log the failure for admin review

EXPECTED RESULT:
- Confirmation email is received within 5-10 minutes of order
- Email content is complete and accurate
- Order details match the purchase
- Email formatting is professional and clear
- Links in email are functional
- Email can be saved as order record
- User's registered email address is used correctly
